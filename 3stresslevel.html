Reactions, When Stress Takes Over

"Sometimes, it's a full storm. Sometimes, it's just a cloud passing by. I've started learning how to name the weather inside me."

I never really rated my stress before.

But while making this journal, I started to notice —

Every day doesn't feel the same.

Some days, I'm slightly off.

Some days, I'm fully down.

And some days, I'm just... floating in the middle.

So I created my own Stress Meter.

🧠 How I Use This in My Life

Each day, I just take a second and ask: "Which zone am I in today?"

  • 🛡️ If I'm at 0 or 1 — I protect that peace.
  • 🛠️ If I'm at 2 or 3 — I try to use my coping tools.
  • ⏸️ If I reach 4 or 5 — I pause everything.

That level needs attention — not hustle.

This stress meter doesn't just help me track the storm. It teaches me to respect my inner weather — even when it's dark.
